    Curran Apple Orchard

    3920 Grandview Dr W University Place, WA 98466

    Distance: 1.3 Miles

    Description

    Cute park and set in the spirit of a food forest in which when the apples are ripe you can pick some. A few apple trees have signs on them asking you not to pick them as each tree is cared for locally which gives them the discretion for this I imagine. There is fun statues and signs going over the history of the park. There is even a small stage where concerts are held. Even if the apples aren't ripe for picking it's still a great park to stroll through.
